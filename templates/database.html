Databases stand as the bedrock upon which countless applications, systems, and businesses rely. A database course delves into the intricacies of organizing, storing, retrieving, and managing data effectively. It's a journey through the evolution of database systems, from the early hierarchical and network models to the robust relational databases of today, and into the realm of NoSQL and NewSQL databases, catering to the diverse needs of contemporary applications. Through this course, students embark on a quest to understand the fundamental concepts such as data modeling, normalization, indexing, and query optimization.
